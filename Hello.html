Hi everyone, this was a fun exercise. Since I live in a suburb to the north of Detroit called Royal Oak, I thought I'd include a few fun facts about the Detroit area.

  1. Detroit is the oldest city west of the original thirteen colonies on this side of the Missippi River
  2. Detroit was the first city to assign individual telephone numbers, before this people had to share use of a party line!
  3. In 1909 the first mile of concrete highway was constructed along Woodward Avenue, between 6 and 7 mile Roads

A few more fun facts

  • The Detroit Lions are unarguably one of the absolute worst professional football teams of all time
  • The Red Wings, on the other hand, have been one of the better Hockey teams, having won more than a few Stanley Cups
  • Unfortuinately we don't have any good soccer teams!
